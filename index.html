What is thermodynamics?

Thermodynamics is a study of effects of heat, work and energy on a system. It gives relation between pressure(P),volume(V) and temperature(T).

There are four laws in the thermodynamics they are,
  • Zeroth law
  • First law
  • Second law
  • Third law

Zeroth law

If the two bodies are each in thermal equilibrium with some third body then they are also in equilibrium with each other

First law

Change in the internal energy is equal to the difference of heat transfer into the system and workdone by the system.

dU = dQ - dW

Perfect engine

If a engine works with 100% efficiency, that is it converts all the heat energy in it to workdone is known as ideal or pefect engine.
