Carnot cycle

An ideal engine operating in a particularly simple cycle known as the carnot cycle. During the cycle process of the engine, some of the heat is absorbed from a hotter reservoir in the isothermal expansion; during the another isothermal compression a small amount of heat is released to a cooler reservoir, So the efficiency of the engine is never goes 100%.Now there are four process in the carnot cycle they are reversible, the carnot engine is a reversible process

The four process are,

  • Isothermal expansion
  • Adiabatic expansion
  • Isothermal compression
  • Adiabatic compression
